UI/UX Designer, Product/Graphic Designer, and Web Developer

From a young age, I was captivated by the power of design and technology to transform experiences. What began as a passion for creating visually stunning artwork evolved into a commitment to crafting digital solutions that enhance user experiences and drive business growth. My journey started at Minas Gerais State University, where I immersed myself in product design, honing my skills in graphic design and understanding the fundamentals of user interaction.

After earning my degree, I dove headfirst into the world of digital design and project management. I took on challenges at companies like Editora Fapi Ltda and Hatashita International, where I led significant projects that not only improved operational efficiency but also elevated user satisfaction. Over the years, my roles evolved, and I found myself at the intersection of creativity and strategy—directing comprehensive digital projects at TVape, Dutchie’s Fresh Market, and Royal Distributing. Each step of my journey has been fueled by a relentless drive to innovate and deliver impactful design solutions.

Today, as an experienced UX/UI Designer and Digital Projects Manager, I bring a unique blend of creativity, technical prowess, and leadership to the table. I specialize in transforming complex concepts into intuitive designs and developing seamless user experiences that captivate and convert. My expertise in web development, project management, and team leadership allows me to tackle challenges head-on and deliver results that exceed expectations.

Whether it's enhancing website functionality, optimizing user interfaces, or managing digital projects from inception to completion, my approach is always user-centric and results-driven. I thrive on collaborating with cross-functional teams and leveraging cutting-edge technologies to create digital solutions that not only meet business objectives but also delight users.
